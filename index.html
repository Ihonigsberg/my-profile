Iris Honigsberg

Hi, my name is Iris Honigsberg

I am a student at HEC Paris. I like tribal and contemporary art, cinema and rugby. I used to be a volunteer in the association La Croix Rouge. Now, I am invested into HEC's associations as Grand Cru, an oenology association or TrHEC, a trekking association. I would like to work in Art Business and more precisely in art finance where innovations interest me a lot.

MY CURRICULUM

La Croix Rouge

La Croix Rouge has more than 65,450 volunteers and 16,703 employees throughout the country. As an auxiliary to the public authorities in its humanitarian missions, La Croix Rouge is constantly fighting to relieve the suffering of people.

Grand Cru

HEC Grand Cru is the wine association of HEC Paris. By organizing weekly tastings on campus and visits to producers, members of the association have the opportunity to meet local producers and discover their products.

TrHEC

The TrHEC association aims to promote the practice of trekking within the HEC Paris business school. It organizes discovery trips and campaigns to raise awareness about the protection of landscapes and nature.
